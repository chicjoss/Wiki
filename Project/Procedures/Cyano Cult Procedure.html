Cyanobacteria Culturing Procedure

NEW CULTURE SETUP:

  • 10ml stock culture to 200ml of fresh medium
  • Place under >100 foot-candle (==1000 lux in SI units) light for 7-10 days
    • Cyanobacteria are easily bleached under higher intensity light (how high?)
    • Once growth is observed, transfer to 50-100 foot-candle area for slower growth
  • Subculture (repeat this process) again after 10 days to 1month
    • Optional: cultures can be shaken at 180-200rpm to enhance culturing

LIGHT LEVELS IN THE LAB (NM measured 20 jun 19):

  • Light intensity in the lab:
    • Benchtop away from windows with lights on: ~20fc
    • On top of yellow cabinet near window edge, overcast day:100-150fc
    • On rack right beside window, overcast day: 700-800 fc
    • In fridge near front: 2-5 fc

PRESERVATION:

  • To be cultured in Allen’s modified blue-green agar
  • Add a layer of culture medium over agar
  • Put into area of around 25foot-candles and about 5-8C below room temp
